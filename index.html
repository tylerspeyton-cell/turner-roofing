San Antonio's Most Trusted Roofer

Since 1914, Turner Roofing Company has been the name San Antonio families and businesses trust when it matters most. What started as a small local operation has grown into one of the area's most respected roofing contractors — and we've done it the old-fashioned way: by showing up, doing excellent work, and standing behind every job we complete.

We are a certified, fully insured roofing contractor serving residential and commercial clients across the greater San Antonio area. Our team brings decades of hands-on experience, and we use only high-quality materials from trusted manufacturers. Whether you need a full roof replacement or a fast repair after a storm, we deliver workmanship that lasts.

Our long-term relationships with customers speak louder than any advertisement. Many of the families we work with today are the second and third generations of clients who first trusted Turner Roofing a lifetime ago.
